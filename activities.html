Activities

Dance

Dancing is one of Dillan's greatest passions. Ever since he was a child, he would always enjoy moving to the songs his parents put on the speakers. He starting pursuing this passion in high school when he joined the break dancing team. After 2 years of break dancing, he decided to take dance and combine it with charitable work. He joined the organization South Asian Culture Show (SACS) where kids from all over the school district came together to perform two shows representing south asian culture in which all the profits would be donated to charities of choice. As president of the club, he managed to raise about $10,000 dollars. Finally, when entering college, he did not plan on stopping his dancing hobby and joined a competitive Bollywood Fusion team known as Zor. After months of practice, his team was able to place 5th in the nation.

Tennis

After trying almost every other sport, Dillan found his most joy in playing Tennis. Starting in 8th grade, Dillan started taking lessons in order to try to get him a spot on his high school team. After joining a league and winning a USTA match with his first coach, he gained more confidence in his abilities and eventually made it onto the Varsity team his sophmore year. When the tennis season was off, Dillan would help coach tennis beginners, which he is currently doing right now.

Dog Rescue

Ever since babysitting a family friend's dog, Dillan's whole family fell in love with them. They decided to give a helping hand by volunteering at a local dog rescure, Holly's Garden Rescue. They would foster dogs and provide them with a cozy and loving stay until they found a forever home. Dillan also used his skills to help manage their website and social media campaigns on Facebook, in order to help advertise the dogs.
